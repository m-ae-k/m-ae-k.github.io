What is Maek

An innovative idea sharing platform that empowers people with ideas to get in touch with people with the skills to make those ideas into a reality. The platform brings together all types of people with all types of experience to create projects in various categories, the best of which are selected for funding. The platform will either fund chosen projects directly or connect them with VC funding, incubators, and capital investment firms.
